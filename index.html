Digital generalist with a penchant for good UX

I’m Patrick, but most people call me Pat. I’m a designer and code-monkey from the Sunshine Coast, Australia.

A little bit of history

For the past 10 years I’ve been working in various digital roles, in-house, in design and marketing agencies and as a freelancer. Although I studied graphic design, my work has been largely web based with a smattering of print and branding work.

One of my most memorable roles to date was at Food Matters where I worked as a designer, front-end dev, general all rounder. I was involved in everything from UI design all the way through to writing API end-points for an iOS app.

Current situation

In 2018 I began teaching User Interface Design (UI) and User Experience (UX) at the University of the Sunshine Coast. It’s a task that I find both incredibly rewarding while at the same time incredibly daunting.

I also currently do some in-house work for the Think Group, where I do a bit of everything from UI design, front-end dev, UX, analytics and reporting, and even a bit of copy writing and digital marketing.

Fun fact about me

When I was a child I was terrified that I would die in a volcanic eruption, until I learned that Australia has no active volcanoes.
